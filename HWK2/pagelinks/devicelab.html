Open Device Lab


A recent introduction to the Polytechnic Campus, The Open Device Lab is a place for those looking for help with web development. Students are able to seek guidance in the creation of mobile applications, Java script utilities,webpage design, and other coding related projets.


Originally founded as a grass-roots community movement the goal of the Open Device Lab is to provide a facility to engage thos working with internet-connected devices and, ultimately work towards improving web applications on the whole.
